What are Student-Led Clubs and Organizations?

UC Santa Cruz has over 150 registered student organizations and associations based on academic, athletic, ethnic, religious, political, and cultural interests.


Student-Led Clubs and Organizations are always here for you.

Students affiliated with UC Santa Cruz are welcome to participate in any Student-Led Clubs and Organizations.
